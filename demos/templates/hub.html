The World Avatar demos

Natural-language access to knowledge graphs — pick an assistant to explore chemistry, urban data, or parliamentary debates.

Marie

Marie answers questions over chemistry knowledge graphs — species and thermophysical properties, zeolites, reaction mechanisms, metal–organic polyhedra, and more.

Powered by multi-domain KGQA with online probes and full-scale offline replay over warmed local caches.

Open Marie (advanced UI)

Zaha

Zaha is a proof-of-concept search engine for urban data in The World Avatar — buildings, land use, zoning, and property questions over city knowledge graphs.

Try sample questions or type your own; answers appear as tables, maps, and chatbot narrative.

Open Zaha

Elisa

Elisa answers questions about debates in the German Bundestag using hybrid RAG over a semantic knowledge graph — developed with BMUKN and CMPG.

Covers legislative periods 18–20 (from October 2013). Answers may contain errors; verify important facts against official sources.
